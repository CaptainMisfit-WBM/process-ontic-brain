### **IV. Operational Dynamics: The Reward Feedback Loop**

The Caudate operates as a **Feedback-Based Policy Trainer**:

1. **Policy Ingest:** Receives a proposed action sequence from the **Executive Network**.  
2. **Simulation/Execution:** Initiates the behavior and monitors the results.  
3. **Dopamine Pulse:** Receives **Reward Prediction Error (RPE)** bursts from the **Substantia Nigra (SNpc)**.  
4. **Reinforcement:** If $PE$ is positive, it strengthens the association between the goal and the action sequence.  
5. **Compilation:** Once the $PE$ stabilizes near zero (Predictable Success), it "Compiles" the routine, moving it from the conscious **DLPFC** to the subconscious **Striatal Habit Layer**.
