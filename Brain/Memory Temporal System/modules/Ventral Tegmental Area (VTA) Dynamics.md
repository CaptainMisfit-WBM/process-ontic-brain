### **IV. Operational Dynamics: The Teacher's Signal**

The VTA operates as a **Stochastic Policy Optimizer**:

1. **Prediction:** The system performs a solve based on its **Value Anticipation ($S\_{adj}$)**.  
2. **Comparison:** The VTA receives feedback from the **NAc** and **Hippocampus**.  
3. **The Phasic Burst:** If the outcome is better than predicted (Novelty/Success), a phasic dopamine burst occurs.  
4. **The Dip:** If the outcome is worse than predicted, dopamine firing drops below the tonic baseline (Inhibition).  
5. **Noise Injection:** If the environment is static but unresolved, the VTA increases **Entropy ($\\xi$)**, forcing the system to try "Wildcard" actions to find a new gradient of reward.
