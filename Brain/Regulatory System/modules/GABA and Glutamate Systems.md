### **IV. Operational Dynamics: The GluD Switch**

The system operates as a **Discrete Temporal Engine**:

1. **Excitation (The Paint):** Glutamate fires, carrying the raw sensory or cognitive content.  
2. **Anchoring (The Anchor):** **GluD receptors** anchor the synapse. If the circuit is "too quiet," GluD activity drops. If "too loud," GluD spikes to regulate the volume.  
3. **Inhibition (The Stencil):** GABA interneurons fire to "cut the tail" of the Glutamate signal. This ensures that thoughts are discrete time steps ($t$) rather than a continuous, muddy flow.  
4. **Ataxia State:** If GluD receptors become hyperactive, the channel stays "stuck open," introducing massive **Noise ($\\xi$)**. This results in **Cognitive Jitter**, where the system cannot hold a steady thought.  
5. **Alignment:** The system uses **Predictive Alignment** to dampen the feedback loop and restore order.
