### **IV. Operational Dynamics: The Retrograde Brake**

The ECS operates as a **Feedback-Driven Circuit Breaker**:

1. **Over-Activation:** A neural circuit (e.g., the Amygdala during stress) becomes hyper-active, releasing high levels of Glutamate.  
2. **Synthesis:** Post-synaptic neurons synthesize lipid-derived messengers (**Anandamide**).  
3. **Retrograde Signal:** These messengers travel *backward* across the synaptic cleft.  
4. **Damping:** They bind to CB1 receptors on the pre-synaptic terminal, telling it to "Slow down" the release of neurotransmitters.  
5. **Equilibrium:** The global state ($\\mathbf{S}$) returns to a homeostatic baseline, preventing excitotoxicity and "Cognitive Burnout."
